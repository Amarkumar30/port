I've always been the person who asks "why?" one too many times. That curiosity led me from an engineering classroom to the world of data science, where every dataset tells a story waiting to be uncovered.

My engineering background gave me the discipline of structured problem-solving. But it was my first encounter with a messy, real-world dataset that truly lit the spark — the thrill of cleaning noise into clarity, of finding the signal that drives a decision. Since then, I've been building models, wrangling data, and chasing that feeling every single day.

I believe great data science isn't just about algorithms — it's about asking the right questions and communicating answers that people can act on. Whether it's predicting customer behavior or visualizing trends that shift strategy, I'm driven to make data useful.
